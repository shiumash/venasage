About the Atlas of Heart Disease and Stroke

At a glance

CDC's Atlas of Heart Disease and Stroke is an online mapping tool that allows users to easily create and customize local-level maps using high-quality data on heart disease and stroke, risk factors, social determinants of health, demographics, and proximity to care.

Get started

Launch the Atlas‎

Visit the Atlas of Heart Disease and Stroke to create your own local-level maps.

Written instructions

Step-by-step instructions for creating maps using the Atlas of Heart Disease and Stroke.

Videos

Video instructions for creating maps using the Atlas of Heart Disease and Stroke.

Applied Atlas videos demonstrating how the Atlas can be used to inform heart disease and stroke prevention programs and policies.
